Publications that are worth keeping an eye out for if you don't already have them

  • * Lamphouse - an annual catalogue of electrical goods and, specifically, radio parts and construction details
  • * The N.Z. Radio Times - a monthly publication in the 30's containing construction details for kits and other radio-related information including DXing
  • * Johns Catalogue - Johns Ltd produced a catalogue (annually?) of radio and electrical goods
