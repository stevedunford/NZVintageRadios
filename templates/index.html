Welcome to the New Zealand Vintage Radio reference site. Our mission is to photo-catalogue as many NZ-designed, manufactured and distributed valve radios (thats tube radios for our American visitors) as possible, preserving them for future generations while also providing a handy reference for restorers and enthusiasts. Alongside this will be the history of those pioneering manufacturers and brands, some of them on the cutting edge of international technological innovation in the radio field.

Much of this information has come from restorers and collectors, from the NZVRS, from old periodicals and newspapers (a special thanks to the team behind PapersPast) - and from the incredible books written by John W. Stokes - The Golden Age of Radio in the Home and More Golden Age of Radio.

A very special thankyou goes to the New Zealand Vintage Radio Society who strive to keep the knowledge and passion for these radios alive. If you're not a member then please consider joining. Membership has its privileges.

Finally a quick note on images:
Many of our vintage radios are becoming hard to find let alone photograph. The images on this site have been assembled from various sources including private and public collections, the web, and online auction sites. It is not our intention to ever 'steal' photos, and is believed that the images used on this site are in the public domain - however if you find a photo that belongs to you then please contact the site admin for attribution, modification or removal.
