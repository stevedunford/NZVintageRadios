Welcome to the New Zealand Vintage Radio reference site. Here you will find information on mainly NZ-designed, manufactured and distributed valve radios, otherwise known as tube radios in the US.

Much of this information has come from restorers and collectors - and from the incredible books written by John W. Stokes - The Golden Age of Radio in the Home and More Golden Age of Radio.

Special thanks goes to the New Zealand Vintage Radio Society who strive to keep the knowledge and passion for these radios alive.

Finally a quick note on images. They have been assembled from many sources - either from enthusiasts collections, the web, and from Trade Me (NZ's version of eBay). It is believed that these images are public domain, however if you find a photo that you took and you want it removed or altered then please contact the site admin.
